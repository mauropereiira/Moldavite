Moldavite

Your thoughts, forged from cosmic impact.

Download for Mac

macOS 10.15+ · Apple Silicon & Intel

Moldavite is a rare green gemstone formed 15 million years ago when a meteorite struck Earth. The impact fused terrestrial and extraterrestrial matter into something entirely new.

This app carries that spirit. Your notes live only on your Mac—fused with your workflow, shaped by your thinking, belonging entirely to you.

No cloud. No accounts. No tracking.

Local-first privacy

Notes stored in ~/Documents/Moldavite/. Your data never leaves your device. Period.

Rich editing

Markdown shortcuts, [[wiki links]], images, code blocks, and task lists.

Daily notes

One click to today's note. Apple Calendar integration in the sidebar.

Connected thinking

Backlinks show what connects to what. Folders when you need structure.
